
#Article: Erich Kellerhals
#Type: regular article

Erich Kellerhals is a German billionaire, co-founder of Media-Saturn-Holding GmbH,owner of Media Markt.

Early life

Career

Erich and Helga Kellerhals, together with Walter Gunz and Leopold Stiefel, co-founded Media-Saturn-Holding GmbH, owner of Media Markt.

Personal life

He is married with one child, and lives in Ingolstadt, Germany.
