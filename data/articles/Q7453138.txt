
#Article: Serena Varghese
#Type: regular article

Serena Beth Varghese (Born October 10, 1981 in Houston, TX) is an American actress and voice actress of Indian descent. She primarily does voice work in anime titles with ADV Films and Seraphim Digital. She and her husband Jorge Alberto Gonzalez currently live in Los Angeles, California.
