
#Article: Olga Kurkulina
#Type: regular article

Olga Kurkulina (אולגה קורקולינה; born 1971) is an Israeli high-jumper, bodybuilder and actress.

In the 2013 movie Kick-Ass 2, she played Mother Russia, a villain.

Personal life

Kurkulina was born in Uzbekistan, then a part of the USSR, in 1971. She emigrated to Israel in later life.

Kurkulina is married and has two children, daughter Anna and son Danila.
