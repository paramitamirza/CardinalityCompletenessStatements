
#Article: Emma Laura
#Type: regular article

Emma Laura () is a Mexican telenovela actress who has not appeared in telenovelas since her role in Marisol in 1996.

Biography

Emma was born in 1971 in Mexico, possibly in Guadalajara. Very little is known about her. Her birth name is Emma Laura Gutiérrez Jiménez.

She played Gabriela in Ángeles blancos, Verónica in Cuando llega el amor with famous Lucero, Isabel Flores Santos in Muchachitas (1991) and Rossana Valverde in Marisol (1996).

Archive footage of her role of Isabel appeared in TV documentary Grandes finales de telenovelas.

She is married and has two children.
