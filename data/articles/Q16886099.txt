
#Article: Reggie Thomas
#Type: regular article

Reginald "Reggie" Thomas (born May 20, 1953) is an American politician from the state of Kentucky. A member of the Democratic Party, Thomas is a member of the Kentucky Senate.

Thomas' election marks the first time that two African Americans will serve in the Kentucky Senate concurrently.

Thomas is an attorney and law professor; he earned an A.B. degree from Dartmouth College in 1975 and a J.D. degree from Harvard Law School in 1978.
