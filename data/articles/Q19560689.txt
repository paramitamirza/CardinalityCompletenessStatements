
#Article: Sayed Ahmad Haqbeen
#Type: regular article

Sayed Ahmad Haqbeen ( سید احمد حقبین,  سید احمد حقبین, born 1953) is the current Acting minister of Ministry of Border & Tribal Affairs of Afghanistan, and a former governor of Kapisa province. He was elected as the Acting minister of Ministry of Border & Tribal Affairs of Ministry of Border & Tribal Affairs on 31 December 2014. He previously served as Deputy Minister and as the Governor of Kapisa province.http://www.mob.gov.af/en/
