
#Article: Zhu Xingliang
#Type: regular article

Zhu Xingliang (; born 1959) is a Chinese entrepreneur, the founder of Suzhou Gold Mantis Construction Decorationn, an interior design and decoration company.

Early life

Zhu Xingliang was born in 1959, and educated at Suzhou University of Science and Technology, from where he graduated in 1987. 

Career

Zhu Xingliang founded Suzhou Gold Mantis Construction Decorationn, an interior design and decoration company. 

Personal life

He is married with one child and lives in Suzhou, China.
