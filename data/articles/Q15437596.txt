
#Article: Nicolae Lupan
#Type: regular article

Nicolae Lupan (born March 16, 1921) is a Bessarabian journalist.

Biography

In 1958, Lupan became the first editor in chief of TeleRadio-Moldova. He was a member of the National Patriotic Front and lost his job. In March 1974, Lupan went into exile in Belgium. In exile he worked for Radio Free Europe (1974-1987). 

Lupan was the president of the Pro-Bessarabia and Bukovina Association (Asociaţia Pro-Basarabia şi Bucovina). The association was formed in Paris on November 27, 1950 by Nicolae Dianu and reactivated by Lupan in 1975.
