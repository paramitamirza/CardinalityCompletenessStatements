
#Article: John Cygan
#Type: regular article

John Cygan is an American actor, voice artist, and comedian.

Early life

Cygan was born in New York City to a large family of twelve children, six of them being half-siblings. His parents never married.He attended West Babylon High School on Long Island and was active in the church theatre troupe while in high school, including a star performance as the insane brother in Arsenic and Old Lace. He was also a talented photographer.

Career

Cygan is notable for his television work (The Commish, The X-Files) and his voice work (Star Wars games, Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty, SOCOM: U.S. Navy SEALs Fireteam Bravo games).
