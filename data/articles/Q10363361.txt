
#Article: Roberto Irineu Marinho
#Type: regular article

Roberto Irineu Marinho (born 1947) is a Brazilian billionaire businessman, the co-owner, chairman and CEO, Grupo Globo.

Early life

Roberto Irineu Marinho was born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, on October 13, 1947, one of three sons of the late Roberto Marinho.

Career

Following the death of his father in 2003, aged 98, he and his brothers inherited control of Grupo Globo, Brazil's largest media group.

According to Forbes, he has an estimated net worth of $6.4 billion as of August 2015.

Personal life

He is married with four children.
