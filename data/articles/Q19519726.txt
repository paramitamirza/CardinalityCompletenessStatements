
#Article: Jude Reyes
#Type: regular article

Jude Reyes (born 1955) is an American businessman, Co-chairman (with his brother J. Christopher Reyes) of Reyes Holdings, a beer and food distribution holding company, which includes the Martin-Brower Company, McDonald's's largest distributor.

Early life

Jude Reyes was born in 1955. He has a degree from Wofford College.

Career

According to Forbes, Reyes has a net worth of $3.9 billion, as of January 2015.

Personal life

Reyes is married with three children and lives in Palm Beach, Florida.
