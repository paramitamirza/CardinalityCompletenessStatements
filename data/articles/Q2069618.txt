
#Article: Letícia Spiller
#Type: regular article

Letícia Pena Spiller (born June 19, 1973 in Rio de Janeiro) is a Brazilian actress.

Personal life

She was married to actor Marcello Novaes, who has a son named Pedro, born in 1996.

On January 20, 2011 the actress gave birth to the girl Stella, her daughter and her boyfriend, Lucas Loureiro. She is with him since June 2009.
