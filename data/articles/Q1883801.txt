
#Article: Magdalena Martullo-Blocher
#Type: regular article

Magdalena Martullo-Blocher is a Swiss businesswoman, the CEO of Ems-Chemie and the daughter of former Swiss Federal Councillor Christoph Blocher

Early life

She is the daughter of Swiss industrialist turned politician, Christoph Blocher, who founded the Swiss chemical company Ems-Chemie.

Career

Martullo-Blocher took over running Ems-Chemie from her father in 2004.

Personal life

She is married with three children and lives in Feldmeilen, Switzerland.
