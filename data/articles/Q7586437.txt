
#Article: Srini Raju
#Type: regular article

Chintalapati Srinivasa Raju, popularly known as Srini Raju, is an Indian Information Technology (IT) Professional, Entrepreneur, and Private Equity Investor. Srini is the Co-founder and Chairman of Peepul Capital (successor to iLabs Venture Capital Fund), a Private Equity (PE) firm based out of Hyderabad and Chennai. Besides funding and mentoring next generation entrepreneurs, he plays an active role in building educational institutions of higher learning.

Education

He is a graduate of National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra

Philanthropic activities

Besides investing and mentoring young professional and entrepreneurs, he is continuing to make large contributions to the cause of building Institutions of Excellence in the field of Management and Technology.
