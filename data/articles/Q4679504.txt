
#Article: Adam Minarovich
#Type: regular article

Adam Minarovich (born January 30, 1977 Houston, Texas) is an American actor, screenwriter and film director. He is known for his recurring role as Ed in the American television series, The Walking Dead.

Minarovich, a native of Anderson, South Carolina, is married with one daughter. He has operated a gold resale and cell phone store with his cousin between acting roles. Minarovich directed and appeared in the film, Exhibit A-7. Most recently, Minarovich portrayed Ed, the abusive husband of Carol (Melissa McBride) in the 2010 television series, The Walking Dead.
