
#Article: Anna Simpson
#Type: regular article

Anna Simpson (born March 23, 1985) is an American actress and singer born in Brooklyn, New York. She is best known for the film, Our Song, alongside Kerry Washington and Melissa Martinez (2000). She has a daughter named Chasity who she gave birth to at the age of 15. Simpson is a survivor of sexual abuse.Most recently, she obtained her associate degree in an automotive school.
