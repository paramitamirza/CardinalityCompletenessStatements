
#Article: Carlos Ocariz
#Type: regular article

Carlos Eduardo Ocariz Guerra (Caracas, Venezuela; 1971) is a Venezuelan politician and engineer. He is the mayor and former deputy of Sucre Municipality in east Caracas. Ocariz is a member of Primero Justicia (Justice First).
