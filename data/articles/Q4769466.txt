
#Article: Annie de Chabert
#Type: regular article

Ansetta de Chabert (also known as Ansetta Muckle de Chabert, Ansetta de Chabert Clarke, Annie de Chabert) (1908-1976), was a St. Croix, Virgin Islands business woman and civic activist. After her death she was honoured by Resolution 778, March 1, 1976 of the Virgin Islands legislature, which commemorated her "lifetime of human warmth, noble pursuits and good works throughout the Virgin Islands community."
