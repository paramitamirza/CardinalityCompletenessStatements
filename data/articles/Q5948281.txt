
#Article: Juan Carlos Escotet
#Type: regular article

Juan Carlos Escotet (born 1934) is a Venezuelan billionaire banker, the founder of Banesco.

Early life

Juan Carlos Escotet has a master's degree from the University of Miami.

Career

Escotet founded Banesco.

Personal life

Escotet is married with four sons, all of whom work at Banesco.
