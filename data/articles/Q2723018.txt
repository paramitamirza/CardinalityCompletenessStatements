
#Article: Chie Nakamura
#Type: regular article

Chie Nakamura (born May 14, 1979), affectionately called Chie-chan (千絵ちゃん), is a Japanese voice actress originally from Tokyo, Japan who works for AXL-One. She is best known as the voice of Sakura Haruno in the Naruto series. She was discovered by a voice actor by the name of Yasuo Muramatsu.
