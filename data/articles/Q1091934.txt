
#Article: Cilia van Dijk
#Type: regular article

Cilia van Dijk-Lieshout (born 22 November 1941 in Uden) is a Dutch film producer. Her film work includes Frieze Frame, I Move, so I Am, Anna & Bella. Anna & Bella earned her a Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film. In 1963 she married Gerrit van Dijk, a Dutch animator.

In 1978 she created Animated People, a distributor for Dutch animation films.
