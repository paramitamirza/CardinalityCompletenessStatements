
#Article: François Audouy
#Type: regular article

François Audouy (born June 17, 1974) is a French-American movie production designer and art director. He designed The Wolverine (2013), and the period epics Abraham Lincoln: Vampire Hunter (2012) and Dracula Untold (2014).

He has worked as an art director for directors Michael Bay, Tim Burton, Martin Campbell, Robert Redford, James Mangold, Zach Snyder, and Steven Spielberg.

Early life

Audouy was born in Toulouse, France, to a French father and an English mother. He moved to Southern California at the age of six after his parents divorced and his mother remarried. He is a bi-lingual speaker of French and English.
