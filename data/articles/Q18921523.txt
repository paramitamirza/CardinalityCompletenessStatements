
#Article: Vanich Chaiyawan
#Type: regular article

Vanich Chaiyawan (born 1931) is a Thai businessman, Chairman of Thai Life Insurance, the second-largest life insurer in Thailand.

Early life

Vanich Chaiyawan was born in 1931. 

Career

Vanich Chaiyawan owns about 60% of Thai Life, which is now run by his son Chai Chaiyawan as President.

Personal life

He is married with eight children and lives in Bangkok.
