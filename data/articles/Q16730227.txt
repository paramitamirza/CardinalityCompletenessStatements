
#Article: Jayashree
#Type: regular article

Jayashree is an Indian actress who has acted in Tamil-language films in the 1980s. She was introduced by C. V. Sridhar in Thendrale Ennai Thodu opposite Mohan in 1985. She got married to a business man in 1988 and has two sons. She quit acting after her marriage. However, she made a comeback with movie Kadhal 2 Kalyanam in 2014, but the movie release has been delayed
