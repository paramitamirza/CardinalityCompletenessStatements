
#Article: Alexandra Lamy
#Type: regular article

Alexandra Lamy (born 14 October 1971) is a French actress.

Personal life

Lamy was married to the Academy Award-winning actor Jean Dujardin. Her sister, Audrey Lamy, is also an actress and currently stars in the French sitcom Scènes de ménage.

Career

From 1999-2003, she starred in Un gars, une fille, alongside her future husband Jean Dujardin.
