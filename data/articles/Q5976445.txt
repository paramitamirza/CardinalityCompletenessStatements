
#Article: Lilo Vilaplana
#Type: regular article

 

Lilo Vilaplana Cuban Director and Writer, also a Teacher and Guionist.

Career

The beginnings of Lilo Vilaplana back in Cuba, from 1988 worked as assistant director in children's series for directors such as Roberto Villar Aleman, Raul Guerra, María Elena Espinosa and Eduardo Macias.He began his career as a screenwriter and director with the Children's live weekly program " Dando Vueltas" from 1991 to 1997, year in which he travels to settle in Colombia .

In Colombia it works as creative in Film and TV Cine company and after several jobs are linked to FoxTelecolombia, a company which achieves greater recognition as Director of the series "El Capo".
