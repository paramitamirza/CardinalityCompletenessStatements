
#Article: Nurit Koren
#Type: regular article

Nurit Koren (נורית קורן, born 24 February 1960) is an Israeli lawyer and politician.

Biography

Born in Jerusalem, Koren worked as the office manager for Gilad Erdan whilst he was Minister of Environmental Protection.

Prior to the 2015 elections she was placed 28th on the Likud list, and was elected to the Knesset as Likud won 30 seats.

Koren has four children and lives in Herzliya.
