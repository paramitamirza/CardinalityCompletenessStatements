
#Article: Kothuku Nanappan
#Type: regular article

Kothuku Nanappan is an Indian theater actor, mimicry artist and film actor in Malayalam movies during the 1980s and 1990s. His popular movies are Naadodikkaattu (1987), Aanavaal Mothiram (1991), Sarapanjaram (1979), Paavam Poornima (1984) and Chenkol (1993). His role as a supervisor role in the comedy film Nadodikattu is well noted for his performance.

Personal life

He was born to Saraswathy Antharjanam and G. Shankaran Potti, of Muttathu Madom, Peruunai, Changanasseri, Kerala, on March 12, 1935. His official name was S. Narayanan Namboothiri.
