
#Article: Reinette Klever
#Type: regular article

R.J. (Reinette) Klever (born July 21, 1967 in Weesp) is a Dutch politician and former asset manager. As a member of the Party for Freedom (Partij voor de Vrijheid) she has been an MP since September 20, 2012. Previously she was a member of the Senate from June 7, 2011 to September 20, 2012.

Reinette Klever lives in Ermelo.
