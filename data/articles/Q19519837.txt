
#Article: Sun Guangxin
#Type: regular article

Sun Guangxin (born 1962) is a Chinese businessman.

Early life

Sun Guangxin was born in 1962. He received a Master of Arts from the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, and an EMBA from Tianjin University.

Career

In 1989, Sun Guangxin founded Xinjiang Guanghui Industry Investment Group. Today, this is a conglomerate with interests on energy, real estate and auto distribution. China Grand Auto, which is controlled by Guanghui Group, is the largest auto dealer in China. 

Personal life

He is married with three children and lives in Urumqi, China.
