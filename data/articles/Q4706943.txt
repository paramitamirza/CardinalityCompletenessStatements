
#Article: Alan J. Folkman
#Type: regular article

Alan J. Folkman is an American businessman who is Private investor and president of Eagle Management LLC. He serves on the board of directors for Albertina Kerr Centers and is the former senior VP and CIO of Columbia Management. He received a B.S. in Business finance in 1967 from the Brigham Young University Marriott School of Management. Folkman served as chair of the National Advisory Council for his alma mater and was honored with its W. Lowell Benson Lifetime Service Award on October 4, 2007.
