
#Article: Lwin Moe
#Type: regular article

Lwin Moe (လွင်မိုး, ; born 1970) is a two-time Myanmar Academy Award winning Burmese film actor.

He was born on an island in the Mergui Archipelago at the southern end of Myanmar. Father of two girls, Lwin Moe was a UNICEF "partner" for Myanmar in 2005. His grandfather is a Punjab (India) origin.

Lwin Moe is usually famous for his fabulous acting roles in the comedy movies. He also used to sing several songs . So, He is a man with different talents with acting, singing and photography. The actor is taking a break from the movie business from around 2005. He will be back to the movie audiences soon.

Lwin Moe's brother, Maung Thi, is a famous director in Myanmar. The actor's wife was one of the TV representatives in Myanmar. His daughter, Yun Wadi Lwin Moe, is also a model and actress. 

Lwin Moe is also an aspiring photographer.
