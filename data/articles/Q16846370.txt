
#Article: Sally Gimson
#Type: regular article

Sally Gimson is a British Labour Party politician, councillor in the Highgate ward of Camden Council.

Until her election to Camden Council she was Head of Public Policy at Victim Support, and she was previously Director of Communications at the Family and Parenting Institute. 

Sally Gimson was an unsuccessful parliamentary candidate in the South Leicestershire constituency in the 2010 General Election.

Sally is married to Andrew Gimson. They have three children.
