
#Article: Michael Kamau
#Type: regular article

Michael Kamau (born 1958) is a Kenyan civil servant who was nominated by President Uhuru Kenyatta as Cabinet Secretary for Transport and Infrastructure on 25 April 2013. He was suspended from the Cabinet on March 28, 2015on allegations of corruption and charged in court with abuse of office on June 4, 2015. He was replaced by James Macharia in an acting capacity.
