
#Article: Filaret Galchev
#Type: regular article

Filaret Galchev (Филарет Гальчев; born 26 May 1963 in Tbilisi, Georgian Soviet Socialist Republic) is a Russian businessman, Chairman of Eurocement group.

Early life

Filaret Illitch Galchev was born in Tbilisi, the capital of Georgia, then a part of the Soviet Union. He graduated from Moscow Mining Institute

Career

According to Forbes, Galchev has a net worth of $4.3 billion, as of January 2015.

Personal life

He is married with two children and lives in Moscow.
