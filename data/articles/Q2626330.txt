
#Article: Maria Ribeiro
#Type: regular article

Maria do Amaral Ribeiro (born November 9, 1975 in Rio de Janeiro) is a Brazilian actress.

Biography

She worked in soap operas such as A Escrava Isaura, playing the beautiful Malvina, and also Luz do Sol, playing Zoé. She also made the film the young Anamaria Tolerância, directed by Carlos Gerbase gaucho.

She had a son with ex-husband Paulo Betti, João, born in 2003. She is married to actor Caio Blat who had his 2nd son, Bento, born in 2010.
