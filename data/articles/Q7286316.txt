
#Article: Rajmonda Bulku
#Type: regular article

Rajmonda Bulku (born in 1958 in Cërrik) is an Albanian actress and a member of the Assembly of the Republic of Albania for the Democratic Party of Albania.

Life

Born in Cërrik in August 16, 1958, Bulku's first part was in the Nusja dhe shtetrrethimi movie, but it was her second movie, The Lady from the City, that gained her national praise. She went on to play in several other movies and is currently artistically active. In the 1990s she started to be involved in politics and became a member of the parliament, representing the Democratic Party of Albania.
