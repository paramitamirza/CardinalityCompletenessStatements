
#Article: Imran Aslam
#Type: regular article

Imran Aslam is a journalist and media personality from Pakistan.

Before he turned to journalism, he worked for Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan of the UAE. He began his journalistic career as the editor of The Star in the 1980s. In the 1990s he was editor of The News, a Karachi English daily. He is currently the president of GEO Television, a subsidiary of the Jang Group.
