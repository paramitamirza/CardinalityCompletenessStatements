
#Article: Peter Nyman
#Type: regular article

Peter Nyman (born 1966) is a Finnish journalist and television reporter. He was the presenter of TV program Uutisvuoto from the show's start in 1998 until 2010. He also hosted Premier league studio in Canal+. Before Uutisvuoto, Nyman was known as a host of Mediapeli. After Uutisvuoto, he works as a news anchor on the MTV3 channel.

In his 2007 book Ankkalammikko (duck pond) he tells about belonging to the Swedish-speaking minority in Finland.
