
#Article: Kunio Busujima
#Type: regular article

Kunio Busujima (extra=born April 1, 1925) is the founder and chairman of Sankyo, one of the three major pachinko machine makers in Japan.

According to Forbes in January 2015, he has an estimated net worth of US$4.2 billion.
