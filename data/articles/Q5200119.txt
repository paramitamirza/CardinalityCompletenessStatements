
#Article: Cynthia Martinez
#Type: regular article

Cynthia M. Martinez (Born September 15, 1975 in Houston, Texas) is an American voice actress who works for ADV Films and Seraphim Digital/Sentai Filmworks. Her first role was that of Lina Inverse in the Slayers movies and OVAs. She is also the lead singer in the Latin band SinTema.
