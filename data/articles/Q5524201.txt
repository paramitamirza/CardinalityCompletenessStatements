
#Article: Garry Pankhurst
#Type: regular article

Garry Pankhurst (born October 1958) is an Australian former child actor, best known for his role as Sonny Hammond in the 1960s Australian children's television series Skippy. He left the acting profession and went into hotel and restaurant management.  He later started a career in meat export to Malaysia which ironically included kangaroo meat, a situation he referred to as "Sonny's revenge".
