
#Article: Henry Rotich
#Type: regular article

Henry K. Rotich (born c. 1969) is a Kenyan civil servant who has been the Head of Macroeconomics at the Treasury since March 2006. He was nominated by President Uhuru Kenyatta as Cabinet Secretary for the National Treasury on 23 April 2013.He has a Masters Degree in Public Administration(MPA) from the Kennedy School of Government at Harvard University.
