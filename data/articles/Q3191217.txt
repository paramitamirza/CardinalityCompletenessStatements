
#Article: Jānis Bordāns
#Type: regular article

Jānis Bordāns (born 21 June 1967 in Balvi) is a Latvian politician and lawyer, former Minister of Justice of the Republic of Latvia.

Bordāns was elected Minister of Justice in July 2012.

Education

Bordāns did graduate Riga Secondary School 39 in 1985. In 1992, he graduated from University of Latvia and got a Master of Laws degree.
