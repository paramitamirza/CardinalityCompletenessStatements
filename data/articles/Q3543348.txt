
#Article: Tirso Cruz III
#Type: regular article

Tirso Cruz III (born Tirso Silvano Cruz III, April 1, 1953) is a Filipino actor and singer. He is one of the famous actors in Philippine show business.

Personal life

He is married to Lynn Ynchausti-Cruz and has two sons and one daughter.
