
#Article: Ivan Vyrypaev
#Type: regular article

Ivan Aleksandrovich Vyrypaev (Ива́н Алекса́ндрович Вырыпа́ев); (born August 3, 1974) is a Russian playwright, screenwriter, film director, actor and art director of the Praktika Theatre in Moscow.
