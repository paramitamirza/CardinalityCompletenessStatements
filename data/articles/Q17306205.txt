
#Article: Bill Longhurst
#Type: regular article

 William Jesse "Bill" Longhurst is the current British Ambassador to Cambodia. He was appointed on 5 June 2013 to succeed Mark Gooding and took office in January 2014. He has worked in the Foreign and Commonwealth Office since 1990.

He is married to Kathryn Scheding and is a father of four children. His wife, and two of his children currently accompany him in Cambodia.
