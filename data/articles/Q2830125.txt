
#Article: Alain Mérieux
#Type: regular article

Alain Mérieux is a French businessman, Chairman of Institut Mérieux.

Early life

Alain Mérieux is the son of Charles Mérieux (1907-2001), and the grandson of Marcel Mérieux (1870-1937), who founded Institut Mérieux in 1897.

Career

According to Forbes, Mérieux has a net worth of $3.5 billion, as of January 2015.

Personal life

He lives in Lyon and is married with three children.
