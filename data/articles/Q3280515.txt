
#Article: Majid Pishyar
#Type: regular article

Majid Pishyar ((مجید پیشیار)born 15 January 1955 in Shiraz) is an Iranian businessman. He is CEO of Dubai-based 32Group and is owner of football clubs, S.C. Beira-Mar (Portugal) and previously FC Admira Wacker Mödling (Austria) and the former owner of Servette FC (Switzerland).
