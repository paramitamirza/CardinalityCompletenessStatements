
#Article: Aura Cristina Geithner
#Type: regular article

 Aura Cristina Geithner (born 9 March 1967 in Bogota, Colombia) is a Colombian actress. In her teens, she lived in Mexico and studied painting and international public relation. Her twin brother, Harry Geithner, is also an actor.
