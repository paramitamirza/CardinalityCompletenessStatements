
#Article: Justin Gleeson
#Type: regular article

Justin Thomas Gleeson SC (born 1961) is the current Solicitor-General of Australia, the Commonwealth's second-ranking law officer.

Early life and education

Gleeson was educated at St Patrick's College, Strathfield then the University of Sydney, from which he graduated with a Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Laws (First-Class Honours). Gleeson undertook postgraduate studies in the law at the University of Oxford, where he earned a Bachelor of Civil Law.

Career

Prior to his appointment as Solicitor-General of Australia, Gleeson was the Head of Banco Chambers.
