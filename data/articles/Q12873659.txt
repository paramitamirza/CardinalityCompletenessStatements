
#Article: Andreas Barkoulis
#Type: regular article

Andreas Barkoulis (Ανδρέας Μπάρκουλης; born 1934) is a Greek actor. He was the most popular male actor of the 1950s and 60s. He was considered such a heartthrob that a popular expression of the era was created for him: "Eh koritsia, o Barkoulis!" (Hey girls, it's Barkoulis!).
