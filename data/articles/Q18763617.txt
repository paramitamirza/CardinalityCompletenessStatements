
#Article: Doug Meijer
#Type: regular article

Douglas Meijer (born 1955) is an American businessman, co-chairman of the US supermarket chain Meijer.

Early life

Doug Meijer is the son of Frederik Meijer, and grandson of Hendrik Meijer, who founded the US supermarket chain Meijer in 1934. He has a degree from the University of Michigan.

Career

According to Forbes, Hank and Doug Meijer have a joint net worth of $9.7 billion, as of January 2015.

Personal life

He lives in Grand Rapids, Michigan.
