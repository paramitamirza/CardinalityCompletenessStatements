
#Article: Payyavula Keshav
#Type: regular article

Payyavula Keshav (born 14 May 1965) was a member of the Legislative Assembly of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh representing the Uravakonda constituency of Anantapur. He is a Politician belonging to the Telugu Desam party He was contested as an MLA four times from Uravakonda Assembly Constituency in Anantapur district.
