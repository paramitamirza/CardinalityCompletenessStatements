
#Article: Alexander Svetakov
#Type: regular article

Alexander Alexandrovich Svetakov (Александр Александрович Светаков; born 15 February 1968 in Moscow) is a Russian billionaire property developer, the founder and chairman of Absolut Group

Early life

Alexander Alexandrovich Svetakov was educated at the Moscow Institute of Electronic Technology.

Career

Svetakov started importing electronics from Singapore, beofre investing in banking. He sold Absolut-bank in 2007 to a Belgian group for almost $1 billion. Since then, he has shifted to property development, and in 2013 was developing five projects in London and New York City.

Personal life

He is divorced with three children and lives in Moscow, Russia.
