
#Article: Herma Kirchschläger
#Type: regular article

Herma Kirchschläger (née Sorger) (15 May 1916 in Vienna  -  30 May 2009 in Vienna) was the wife of Rudolf Kirchschläger, the former minister of foreign affairs and later federal president of Austria. They married in 1940 and they had two children: Christa (1944) and Walter (1947).
