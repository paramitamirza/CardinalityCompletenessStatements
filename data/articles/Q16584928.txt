
#Article: Juan Manuel Moreno Bonilla
#Type: regular article

Juan Manuel Moreno Bonilla (born 1 May 1970) is a Spanish politician and member of the People's Party, currently President of the PP Andalusian branch. He represented Cantabria Province in the Spanish Congress of Deputies from 2000 to 2004 and Malaga Province from 2007 to 2011. He has also been a member of the Senate of Spain and Parliament of Andalusia.
