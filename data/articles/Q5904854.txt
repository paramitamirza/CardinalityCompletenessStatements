
#Article: Humberto Aguilar Coronado
#Type: regular article

Marco Humberto Aguilar Coronado (born 25 March 1963) is a Mexican politician affiliated to the National Action Party. As of 2014 he served as Senator of the LX and LXI Legislatures of the Mexican Congress representing Puebla. He also served as Deputy between 1991 and 1994
