
#Article: Luke Hemsworth
#Type: regular article

Luke Hemsworth (born 1981) is an Australian actor who is best known for his role as Nathan Tyson in the TV series Neighbours.

Early life

Luke Hemsworth was born in Melbourne, Australia on November 6, 1980 to Leonie, an English teacher, and Craig Hemsworth, a social-services counselor. He is the eldest of three sons. His brothers are actors Chris and Liam Hemsworth.

Career

Hemsworth started his career on the Australian soap opera Neighbours as Nathan Tyson. Mainly a television actor, Hemsworth has appeared in TV series such as The Saddle Club, Blue Heelers, Last Man Standing, All Saints, and Satisfaction. In 2012, he starred in the 6-part miniseries Bikie Wars: Brothers in Arms as Gregory "Shadow" Campbell. He is set to star in the upcoming Australian War Film The 34th Battalion as Robinson.
