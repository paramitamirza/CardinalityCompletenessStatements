
#Article: Madhusudan Yadav
#Type: regular article

Madhusudan Yadav was a member of the 15th Lok Sabha of India. He represented the Rajnandgaon constituency of Chhattisgarh and is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). He won this seat, defeating his Congress rival Devvrat Singh by a margin of more than 100,000 votes

Education & background

Yadav has received education only till Higher Secondary.BA

Involvement with Jan Lokpal Bill

In December 2011, Madhusudan Yadav advocated inclusion of Group C employees under the proposed Lokpal Bill
