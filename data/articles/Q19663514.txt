
#Article: Murat Ülker
#Type: regular article

Murat Ülker (born 1955) is a Turkish businessman, chairman of Ülker and Yıldız Holding, Turkey's largest food producer.

Early life

Murat Ülker was born in 1959 and is a graduate of Bogazici University. He is a descendant of the founders of Ülker, a Turkish manufacturer of biscuits, crackers, and chocolates that was founded in 1944 by Sabri Ülker and Asim Ülker.

Career

According to Forbes, Ülker has a net worth of $4.0 billion, as of January 2015.

Personal life

He is married with three children and lives in Istanbul.
