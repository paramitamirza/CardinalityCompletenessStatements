
#Article: Pang Chun-hoi
#Type: regular article

Pang Chun-hoi  (born 26 June 1921, Hong Kong) was a member of the member of the Legislative Council of Hong Kong (1985-97) for the Labour (constituency) constituency, representing the Hong Kong and Kowloon Trades Union Council, a pro-Kuomintang union, and Cotton Industry Workers' General Union.
