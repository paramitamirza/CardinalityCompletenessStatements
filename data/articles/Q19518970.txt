
#Article: Ludwig Merckle
#Type: regular article

Ludwig Merckle (born 1964) is a German businessman.

Early life

Ludwig Merckle is the son of Adolf Merckle, who founded Merckle Group. 

Career

According to Forbes, Merckle has a net worth of $4.6 billion, as of January 2015.

Personal life

Merckle is married with two children and lives in Ulm.
