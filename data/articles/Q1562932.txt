
#Article: Dubravka Mijatović
#Type: regular article

Dubravka Mijatović (Serbian: Дубравка Мијатовић; born 27 December 1968 in Belgrade) is a Serbian actress. She's acted since she was a teenager. Recently, she is famous for playing Dragorad's wife in Moj rodjak sa sela RTS TV series and a godmother in Bela ladja RTS TV series.
