
#Article: Emma Kari
#Type: regular article

Emma Kari (born May 15, 1983 in Espoo) is a Finnish politician representing the Green League. She was elected to the Parliament of Finland in the 2015 parliamentary election with a vote total of 4,647. Kari has been in the City Council of Helsinki since 2008.

Kari and Kukka Ranta have written a book Kalavale, which addresses the consequences of commercial fishing.
