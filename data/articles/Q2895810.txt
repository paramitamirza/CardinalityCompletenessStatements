
#Article: Batsheva Kanievsky
#Type: regular article

Batsheva Esther Kanievsky (בת-שבע קנייבסקי; February 1, 1932 — October 15, 2011) was a well known rebbetzin from Bnei Brak, Israel. She was the wife of Rabbi Chaim Kanievsky and oldest daughter of Rabbi Yosef Shalom Eliashiv of Jerusalem. Her grandfather was Rabbi Aryeh Levin. Several hundred people would visit her apartment every week. She never left Israel. She was known as a "miracle worker" and more than 50,000 people attended her funeral.
