
#Article: Luo Jye
#Type: regular article

Luo Jye (born 1925) is a Taiwanese billionaire, founder of Cheng Shin Rubber, the world's ninth largest tire manufacturer.

Early life

Luo Jye was born in 1925.

Career

Luo Jye founded Cheng Shin Rubber in 1967. In January 2015, passed control of Cheng Shin to his son, Lo Tsai-Jen.

According to Forbes, Luo Jye has a net worth of $4.2 billion, as of January 2015.

Personal life

He is married with four children and lives in Ta-Tsun, Taiwan.
