
#Article: O. S. Manian
#Type: regular article

O.S. Manian is a member of the 15th Lok Sabha of India. He represented the Mayiladuthurai constituency of Tamil Nadu and is a member of the All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam political party.

Education & background

Manian has completed formal education only till XII standard. An agriculturist by profession, he has held various offices since 1995.
