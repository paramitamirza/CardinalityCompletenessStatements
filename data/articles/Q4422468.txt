
#Article: Andrei Skoch
#Type: regular article

Andrei Skoch (born 1966) is a Russian businessman, part owner of the steelmaker Lebedinsky Mining.

Early life

Andrei Skoch was born near Moscow, Russia. He was educated at the Institute of Physical Culture.

Career

He worked for MontazhSpetsBank before joining fellow billionaire Alisher Usmanov in the metals business. In 1999, he became a member of the Russian parliament's lower house (State Duma). According to Forbes, Skoch has a net worth of $6.3 billion, as of January 2015.

Personal life

He is divorced with eight children and lives in Moscow.
