
#Article: Kim Sung-kyun
#Type: regular article

Kim Sung-kyun (born May 25, 1980) is a South Korean actor. Kim began his career in theatre, then made a memorable screen debut as a gangster boss's faithful henchman in Nameless Gangster: Rules of the Time, followed by supporting roles as the serial killer in The Neighbor, and a college student who looks older than his age in TV series Reply 1994.
