
#Article: Eero Aho
#Type: regular article

Eero-Matti Aho (born 26 January 1968) is a Finnish actor. He has appeared on stage, in films and on television.

Selected filmography

	Käsky (2008)

	Hyvä poika (2011)

	8-pallo (2013)
