
#Article: Lalubhai Patel
#Type: regular article

Lalubhai Babubhai Patel (born 31 August 1955) is an Indian politician, belonging to Bharatiya Janata Party. In the 2009 election he was elected to the 15th Lok Sabha from the Daman and Diu constituency. Again in the 2014 election he was re-elected to the 16th Lok Sabha from the same constituency.

Personal life

Lalubhai Babubhai Patel was born to Shri Babubhai G. Patel & Smt. Chandanben B. Patel and is married to Smt. Taruna L. Patel. He has 1 son(s) 2 daughter(s)
