
#Article: Yulian Vergov
#Type: regular article

Yulian Emilianov Vergov (Юлиан Емилиянов Вергов; born 15 July 1970) is a Bulgarian actor. His name is also sometimes transliterated as Julian Emilianov Vergov or Yuliyan Emilijanov Vergov.

Prior to turning to acting, Vergov took four years of engineering studies. After that he graduated from theatrical college "Lyuben Greus", where he honed his acting skills under the guidance of Professor Tsvetana Maneva. Some of his famous roles are in the comedy film Mission London and in the drama and romantic series Revolutcia Z.

Vergov is in a long-term relationship with Irina and they have a daughter named Alena.
