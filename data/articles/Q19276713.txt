
#Article: Niels Peter Louis-Hansen
#Type: regular article

Niels Peter Louis-Hansen (born 1944) is a Danish businessman, Deputy chairman and owner of one-fifth of the medical device company Coloplast.

Early life

Niels Peter Louis-Hansen was born in Denmark, the son of Aage Louis-Hansen. He has a B Com degree.

Career

Coloplast was founded in 1957 by his father.
