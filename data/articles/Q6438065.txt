
#Article: Kristiina Elstelä
#Type: regular article

Kristiina Elstelä (born 10 January 1943 in Helsinki, Finland) is a Finnish actress, who has acted in many TV shows and movies. She also has a long career in theater and she is a renowned cabaret artist.

In 2006 Elstelä was one of the competitors in Dancing with the Stars television series. She and her partner Marko Keränen were ranked seconds, right after Tomi Metsäketo and Sanna Hirvaskari.

She is daughter of actor Ossi Elstelä and actress Irja Elstelä.
