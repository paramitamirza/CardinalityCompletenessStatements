
#Article: Paul Bazely
#Type: regular article

Paul Bazely is an English actor, perhaps best known for his performances as Troy in the comedy series Benidorm.His other TV credits include: Making Out, Heartbeat, Doctors, Holby City, Vanity Fair, The IT Crowd, Footballers Wives and, more recently, in the Doctor Who episode, The Doctor, the Widow and the Wardrobe.

Bazely has also appeared in the film Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides as the pirate Salaman.

He currently resides in Brighton.
