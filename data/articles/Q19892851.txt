
#Article: Jiang Bin
#Type: regular article

Jiang Bin (姜滨) is a Chinese billionaire entrepreneur, who co-founded GoerTek, the acoustic components company, with his wife Hu Shuangmei.

GoerTek is listed on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange, and its customers include Samsung, Sony and Lenovo. His brother, Jiang Long, is vice chairman.
