
#Article: Katharina Schüttler
#Type: regular article

 Katharina Schüttler (born 20 October 1979) is an award winning German television and film actress. She is best known internationally for leading roles as Clara Rosenbaum in The Promise (2011) and as Greta Müller in the television drama Generation War (2013).

Biography

She grew up in Cologne. Her father is an actor, director and former theatre director and her mother is a playwright. After high school she studied acting at the Hanover University of Music, Drama and Media from 1999 to 2003.
