
#Article: James Hargrove
#Type: regular article

James "Jim" Hargrove (born October 5, 1953) is an American politician who is a member of the Washington State Senate, representing the 24th District (Hoquiam). He is a Democrat.

Hargrove has been a member of the State Senate since 1993. He had previously served in the Washington House of Representatives from 1985 to 1992.

Hargrove is a member of the Family Policy Council, Children's Oversight, Child Safety for Children in Child Protective Services or Child Welfare Services, Judiciary, and Natural Resources & Marine Waters committees. He is the chair of the Human Services and Corrections and Western Legislative Forestry Task Force committees.
