
#Article: Piet Steenkamp
#Type: regular article

Petrus Antonius Josephus Maria Steenkamp (born 8 March 1925 in Uithoorn, North Holland) is a Dutch politician.

He is a member of the Christian Democratic Appeal (CDA). He played an important role in the formation of the party and is therefore referred to as the father of the CDA. He was president of the Dutch Senate from 1983 to 1991. He was succeeded by Herman Tjeenk Willink.

Decorations

	: Commander of the Order of the Netherlands Lion

	: Commander of the Order of Orange-Nassau
