
#Article: François-Xavier Demaison
#Type: regular article

François-Xavier Demaison (born 22 September 1973 in Asnières-sur-Seine) is a French actor and humorist.

Personal life

Both his parents were lawyers.

On 11 September 2001, Demaison witnessed the attack on the World Trade Center from an office in Manhattan, where he had an internship. After this traumatic event, he decided not to continue on this path mapped out and returns to his old love, the theater.
