
#Article: Magnús Gissurarson
#Type: regular article

Magnús Gissurarson (c. 1175-1236) was an Icelandic Roman Catholic clergyman, who became the eighth bishop of Iceland from 1216 to 1237. He served in the diocese of Skálholt.

Magnús Gissurarson was married and had two sons.
