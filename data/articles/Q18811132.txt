
#Article: Gong Hongjia
#Type: regular article

Gong Hongjia (born 1964/1965) is a Hong Kong businessman, vice-chairman and the largest shareholder of Hikvision, a security equipment supplier. He was ranked 23 in the Forbes 2015 China Rich List, with a net worth of over $5.1 billion.

Early life

Gong Hongjia was born in China in 1964/1965. He was edcuated at Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

Career

Gong Hongjia is a Hong Kong-based investor.

Personal life

He is married with two children and lives in Hong Kong.
