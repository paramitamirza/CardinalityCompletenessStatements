
#Article: Allah Nazar Baloch
#Type: regular article

Dr Allah Nazar Baloch 2 October 1968 is a Baloch political activist.

Early life

Allah Nazar Baloch was born in 1968 in Mashkai, Awaran District, Pakistan.

Career

Baloch served as the Chairman of the Baloch Students Organization. He served as the chief of the Baluch Liberation Front until 2015. 

Baloch's brother and nephew were also members of the BLF. They were killed in Mashkai in June 2015.
