
#Article: Paco Tous
#Type: regular article

Francisco Martínez "Paco" Tous (born on 10 February 1964 in Puerto de Santa María, Cádiz, Andalucía, Spain) is a Spanish actor, born in Puerto de Santa María. He is best known for his starring role as Paco in the Spanish television series Los hombres de Paco.
