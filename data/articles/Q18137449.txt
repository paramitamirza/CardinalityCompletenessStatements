
#Article: Ulla Löfvén
#Type: regular article

Ulla Margareta Löfvén (; born 29 May 1951) is a Swedish politician. She is the wife of Prime Minister Stefan Löfven, who is also leader of the Social Democrats.

She first met Stefan Löfven when they both worked at Hägglunds & Söner in 1992; he worked as an welder and she was representative for the local branch of an trade union. She was married and had two children when they met. They married in November 2003.

She was employed as the assistant of Carin Jämtin, the party secretary of the Social Democrats, until her husband was elected party leader in January 2012.
