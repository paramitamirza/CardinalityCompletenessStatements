
#Article: Henry Cadogan
#Type: regular article

Henry Cadogan (1642-1713/14) of Liscarton was the son of Major William Cadogan and father of William Cadogan, 1st Earl Cadogan (1672-1726) and Charles Cadogan, 2nd Baron Cadogan (1685-1776).

Educated at Trinity College, Dublin, he was a barrister and also High Sheriff of Meath in 1700. He married, on about 31 July 1671, Bridget, daughter of Sir Hardress Waller, by whom he had five children:

	William Cadogan, 1st Earl Cadogan (1672-1726)

	Ambrose Cadogan (d unm 1693)

	Charles Cadogan, 2nd Baron Cadogan (1685-1776)

	Frances Cadogan (d aged 9)

	Penelope Cadogan, who married Sir Thomas Prendergast, 1st Baronet
