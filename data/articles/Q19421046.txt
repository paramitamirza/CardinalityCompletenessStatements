
#Article: Leslee Udwin
#Type: regular article

Leslee Udwin (b. 1957) is a British filmmaker, actress, and producer. 

Early life

She was born in Savyon, Israel, to a Jewish family. 

Career

Udwin started her career as an actor and initially acted in the British soap opera Eldorado. She started her production company, Assassin Films, in 1989. Her well-known productions include the films East is East (1999), Mrs Ratcliffe's Revolution (2007), and West is West (2010), and the documentary India's Daughter (2015).

Personal life

She lives in London, UK.
