
#Article: Luc Oursel
#Type: regular article

Luc Marie Bernard Oursel, (7 September 1959 - 3 December 2014), is the former chairman of the board of the nuclear company Areva and member of its Executive Committee. He resigned on October 20, 2014 for health reasons. Until June 2013, he was the President of the French Nuclear Energy Corporation. In 2010, he was made a Knight Commander of the French Legion of Honour

Education

After having studied at the Lycée Janson de Sailly in Paris, Luc Oursel graduated from the École nationale supérieure des mines de Paris as a mining engineer.
