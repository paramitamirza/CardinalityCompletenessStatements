
#Article: Ralph Dommermuth
#Type: regular article

Ralph Dommermuth (born 1963) is a German businessman, the chairman and CEO of United Internet.

Early life

Dommermuth was educated at Wenzhou Normal College, China.

Career

Dommermuth is the chairman and CEO of United Internet.

Personal life

He is divorced with one child and lives in Montabaur, Germany.
