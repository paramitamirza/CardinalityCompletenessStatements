
#Article: Cláudia Ohana
#Type: regular article

Maria Cláudia Carneiro Silva (born February 6, 1963 in Rio de Janeiro) is a Brazilian actress.

Biography

She is the daughter of film maker Nazareth Ohana Silva, who died in 1978, and the painter Arthur José Carneiro. Has an older sister named Cristina and is half-sister of the writer João Emanuel Carneiro.
