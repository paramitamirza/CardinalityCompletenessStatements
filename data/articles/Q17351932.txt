
#Article: Shawn Fonteno
#Type: regular article

Shawn D. Fonteno (born April 8, 1968), also known as Solo, is an American actor, rapper and recording artist who is best known as the voice and the acting performer of Franklin Clinton from the video game Grand Theft Auto V. Fonteno's performance in Grand Theft Auto V was mainly recorded using motion capture technology.

Early life

Shawn was born on April 8, 1968 in South Los Angeles, California. He is the older cousin of Young Maylay, the voice of Carl Johnson and also a rapper.

Acting career

Fonteno has also acted in the film The Wash.
