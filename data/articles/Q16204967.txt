
#Article: Stephanie Gosk
#Type: regular article

Stephanie Gosk (born 1971) is an American journalist and correspondent who works for NBC News based in New York City. Gosk contributes to all platforms of NBC News, including Today, NBC Nightly News and MSNBC.

Personal life

Gosk and her partner, Weekend Today news anchor Jenna Wolfe, have two daughters, Harper Estelle (b.2013) and Quinn Lily (b.2015).
