
#Article: Junko Takeuchi
#Type: regular article

Junko Takeuchi (born April 5, 1972 in Saitama Prefecture) is a Japanese voice actress employed by Ogipro The Next Co. Inc. Taking a well-trod path by many voice actresses, she often voices young male characters with generally very quirky and goofy personalities. Her most notable roles are in Naruto, where she plays the main character, Naruto Uzumaki, in Medabots as Metabee and in Inazuma Eleven as Mamoru Endou, and in the 1999 version of Hunter x Hunter as Gon Freecss. She is also the Japanese voice of Gumball Watterson on The Amazing World of Gumball. She has a son born in 2012.
