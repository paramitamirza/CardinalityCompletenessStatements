
#Article: Michelle Rojas
#Type: regular article

Michelle Rojas (born December 16, 1987) is an American voice actress affiliated with Funimation from Dallas, Texas. Some of her major roles include Toka Yatogami / Princess in Date A Live, Nashiro Yasuhisa in Tokyo Ghoul, and Toka Yada in Assassination Classroom. She is the wife of Funimation's social media manager Justin Rojas.
