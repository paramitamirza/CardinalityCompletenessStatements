
#Article: Chan Kwok-keung
#Type: regular article

Chan Kwok-keung, JP (born 17 January 1946, Huizhou, Guangdong, China) was the member of the Legislative Council of Hong Kong in 1998-2004 for the Labour constituency. He was the vice-chairman of the Hong Kong Wearing Apparel Industry Employees' General Union, under the pro-Beijing Hong Kong Federation of Trade Unions. He is also the member of the Democratic Alliance for Betterment of Hong Kong.
