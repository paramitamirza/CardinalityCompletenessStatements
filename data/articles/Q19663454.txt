
#Article: Suh Kyung-bae
#Type: regular article

Suh Kyung-Bae (born 1963) is a South Korean businessman. He is the head and owner of AmorePacific, a Seoul-based cosmetics maker and perfumer founded by his father Suh Seong-hwan in 1945.

Early life

Suh Kyung-Bae was born in South Korea in 1963, the son of Suh Seong-hwan. He was educated at Yonsei University.

Career

Suh inherited AmorePacific from his father. According to Forbes, Suh has a net worth of $7.2 billion as of March 2015.

Personal life

Suh is married with two children and lives in Seoul.
