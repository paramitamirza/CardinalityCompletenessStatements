
#Article: Caterina Lopez
#Type: regular article

Caterina Marie Lopez (born December 13, 1987), is an American model and actress. 

Career

In 2011, she did two item songs in the Bollywood film Bhindi Baazaar Inc.

In August 2015, Lopez announced the start of a new project with her partner JJ Hamblett, called "We are the Hambletts." 

Personal life

On September 18, 2013, JJ Hamblett shared on Twitter that he and Caterina are expecting their first baby together. Caterina gave birth to a baby boy, Princeton J. Alexander on November 28, 2013.

Lopez has implied that she is the cousin of actress and singer Jennifer Lopez, however, there has never been any corroborating statements from Jennifer Lopez.
