
#Article: Sanni Grahn-Laasonen
#Type: regular article

Sanni Grahn-Laasonen (born May 4, 1983) is a Finnish politician and a member of the National Coalition Party. She has been the Minister of Education and Culture since 29 May 2015. She was the Minister of the Environment in Alexander Stubb's cabinet between 2014 and 2015.
