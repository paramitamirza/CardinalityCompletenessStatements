
#Article: Mélanie Thierry
#Type: regular article

Mélanie Thierry (born 17 July 1981 in Saint-Germain-en-Laye, Yvelines) is a French actress. She played Aurora in the 2008 film Babylon A.D.

Biography

Mélanie Thierry began her career as a model in France, then moved into acting. She began with a series of roles in French productions, and at the age of 18 appeared in the internationally distributed The Legend of 1900. She also appeared opposite Rufus Sewell in two episodes of the BBC costume drama Charles II: The Power and The Passion, playing the king's French mistress Louise de Kérouaille.

Thierry made her Hollywood debut in the 2008 film Babylon A.D., as Aurora.

Personal life

Thierry has a son named Roman, born 24 May 2008 with long-term partner Raphaël Haroche.
