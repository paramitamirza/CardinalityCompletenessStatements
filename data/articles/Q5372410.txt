
#Article: Emily Warfield
#Type: regular article

Emily Warfield (born August 10, 1972) is an American actress who made her acting debut with the TV movie Dream Date in 1989. She also co-starred in the movie The Man in the Moon with the then-unknown Reese Witherspoon in 1991.

Since then, she has appeared in a variety of guest roles on TV shows, from Doogie Howser, M.D. to L.A. Law. She even appeared in the last two Bonanza TV movies that aired on NBC in the early 1990s as Sarah, a member of that series' iconic Cartwright family.

Her most recent role was in a TV movie called Operation Cupcake (2012).
